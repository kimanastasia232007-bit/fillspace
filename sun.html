1. Why do businesses use programming?

Businesses use programming mainly to automate tasks and processes.

  • Generate reports automatically.
  • Send emails or invoices on a schedule.
  • Update prices or stock levels without manual work.

2. Example of automation

Good example: a script that sends invoices to customers automatically every day.

Bad example: printing all invoices and sending them manually by mail.

3. CSV as data format

CSV (Comma-Separated Values) is a simple text format for tables. It is often used for business data exchange (between Excel, databases, systems).

name,price,quantity Apple,1.20,10 Orange,0.80,5
